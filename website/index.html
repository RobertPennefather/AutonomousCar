AUTONOMOUS CAR

Features

The thought-out design and implementation of this car provides users with many features and ways to use the product in their very own home

Crash Free

When about to hit a wall the car will readjust by turning away. It will also attempt to stay at a fixed distance while driving along the wall.

Follows Walls

Maintains a set distance between the wall and the car by using bang-bang position control. Can also follows a wall around a 90 degree corner.

Cheap

The product was creating using parts anyone can buy from local electronics shops, making the design fully modular and cheap to fix if any parts were to break after purchase.

Fast

The car is able to fly around corners and walls all from the press of the on button. Set it up and it's ready to go!
